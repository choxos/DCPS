About DCPS

The Dental Caries Population Studies (DCPS) project is a comprehensive systematic review and analysis of dental caries prevalence in Canada from 1990 to 2025, with projections to 2050 using advanced Bayesian hierarchical modeling.

Our database includes DMFT/dmft data from studies across all Canadian provinces and territories, enabling researchers and policymakers to understand trends, identify high-burden populations, and inform evidence-based interventions.

This project is part of the Xera DB research ecosystem, dedicated to advancing evidence-based healthcare through comprehensive data analysis and visualization.
